Bank Detail Report

This Online Banking report shows statement and transaction information for a specific bank account statement, including bank statement header information, statement line detail, and reconciled transaction detail.

Functionalities:

  1. Login with your Password.
  2. Enable the customer to check their balance.
  3. Transfer funds.
  4. Pay bills without visiting Bank

Account Related other info:

  • Account Type
  • Account Number
  • MICR
  • IFSC
